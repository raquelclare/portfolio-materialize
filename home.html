APPROACH

I understand that each project is unique and I make sure to tailor to your wants and needs as the client. My approach to web developement is to create clean, efficient, and intuitive code. My own personal style is minimalistic and fresh.





ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Raquel! I am a 26 year old living in Austin, Texas following my passions in web developement. When I'm not coding I catch up on all of the world happenings. Growing up I was an Army brat- both parents. My father is from the ol' USA and my mother is from Spain. I have 2 sisters and 2 brothers, so family means everything.

I graduated in 2013 from the University of Texas at Austin with a BA in International Relations & Global Studies, with a minor in European Studies. Culture, Media & the Arts was my concentration and I completed a study abroad program in Wuerzburg, Germany.

I came into the world of coding pretty slowly- I was introduced to it by my previous company, The Honest Company, where I assisted their tech team in executing very basic quality assurance tests. I started on a more serious path by attending mini seminars and meet-ups hosted through Meet-Up, specifically Women Who Code. From there I enrolled into the Hack Reactor 4-week immersion prep and acquired a basic understanding Javascript. Once that was over, I made the leap into enrolling into a part-time full stack web developer program hosted by UT with ciriculum by Trilogy Education. It happens to be the best decision I ever made.
